Pokhara - The City of Lakes

Pokhara is a beautiful city in Nepal, known for its stunning mountain views, peaceful lakes, and adventure sports. It is one of the most popular travel destinations in the country and offers something for everyone.

Main Attractions in Pokhara

From boating on Phewa Lake to paragliding over the Annapurna Range, Pokhara is a hub for nature lovers and thrill seekers alike.

Popular Activities

  • Boating on Phewa Lake
  • Visiting Davis Falls
  • Exploring Mahendra Cave

Top 3 Adventure Sports

  1. Paragliding
  2. Zip-lining
  3. Trekking to Sarangkot
